Hotel description

The Kiev Hotel, excellent for accommodating guests, is conveniently located in one of the busiest and most notable areas of the city. The hotel is surrounded by impressive high-rise buildings, as well as various restaurants and shopping centers. The hotel is close to the beach. All rooms are equipped with a flat-screen cable TV. They also have a work desk, tea / coffee facilities and a sofa bed suitable for a child. The modern bathroom has a powerful rain shower and a hairdryer.
